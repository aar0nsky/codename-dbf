I'm Getting Too Old For This Sh!t: A Retirement Calculator

Hi it's me, your hard-earned money. You and I are going to grow old together! Using the input fields and buttons below, we will simulate 50 years of savings and growth. The numbers may start out small, but if we keep at it we can try to retire as millionaires!

To begin fill in the fields with the amount of money you can save each month right now, as well as the total balance of your account. These numbers will give us a good idea of where we're at and where we would like to go.

Once you have filled those fields out, click the Good Thing button or the Bad Thing button to simulate 10 years of savings and growth. After five clicks, we'll see how close we are to the retirement of our dreams!
